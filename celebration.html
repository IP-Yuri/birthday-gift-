My Dearest,

Happy Birthday. I am endlessly grateful to share this day with you, to stand beside you and marvel at the person you are becoming. It is a true privilege to bear witness to your essence, to see your growth, your quiet resilience, and the delicate sweetness you carry within. I have watched you navigate your struggles, turning hardship into something so graceful, making the impossible look entirely effortless. I am so incredibly proud of you.

I want you to know, in your very core, how deeply I value you, care for you, and love you. You deserve a love that is unwavering, and you deserve to hear it spoken. I want you to know that you are never alone and forever taken care of. Whether you are smiling or sorrowful, soaring in victory or feeling lost, whether you feel whole or broken, you are the one I choose to show up for. I am committed to healing and growing, not just for myself, but to be the gentle, understanding harbor you deserve. You are worthy of being treated with absolute tenderness and depth.

No matter the shadows you might see in yourself, or how difficult you may sometimes feel you are to be loved, please know this: loving you is the most natural thing in the world. It requires no pretense, only genuine devotion. You are so entirely easy to love because your soul is just that pure—a truly wingless angel.

I know the world has not always been kind, forcing you to forge armor, to be sharp and unapproachable just to protect your tender heart. I am in awe of the fierce bravery it took to survive on your own. But my dear, you can lay that armor down now. You are no longer alone. You can lean on me, run to me, and rest. You are entirely safe here.

As you step into this new year, I wish you profound peace, boundless success, and the realization of every goal you hold dear. I believe in you with every fiber of my being. Go after your desires without the fear of falling; you are meant for greatness, and no effort of yours will ever be in vain. The rewards waiting for you are so vast they will bring you to tears of joy. I have that much absolute faith in your light.

Enjoy your birthday to the absolute fullest. It is your day—a celebration as singular, stunning, and deeply special as the person you are.

With all my love,
